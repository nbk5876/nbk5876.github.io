NextDoor Message Scope

Which ND Groups can see your Post?

There are two groups on NextDoor that can see messages posted to the Pet Lost and Found. One group is members of the Pet Lost and Found.  The other group is "Anyone on NextDoor". Depending on how they are posted, messages can be seen by 1) the Pet Lost and Found or, 2) by anyone on NextDoor or, 3) by both of these groups

1. Only members of Pet Lost and Found.

2. Anyone on Nextdoor.

3. Anyone on Nextdoor and any member of the Pet Lost and Found.
